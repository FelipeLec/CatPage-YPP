Cats

The cat is a domestic species of small carnivorous mammal. It is the only domesticated species in the family and is often referred to as the domestic cat to distinguish it from the wild members of the family. A cat can either be a house cat, a farm cat or a feral cat; the latter ranges freely and avoids human contact.
